# PlanetPulse

**Modular sustainability and compliance platform for enterprises.**

PlanetPulse is a unified SaaS platform helping enterprises, ESG consultants,
and compliance teams manage sustainability reporting, carbon accounting,
risk assessment, and operational compliance — all from a single integrated
workspace.

## What we do

- **Carbon accounting & LCA** — measure and report Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions
- **Risk & compliance** — track regulatory obligations and audit readiness
- **Operational reporting** — unified data layer across sustainability domains
- *More modules in development*

## Built for

- Sustainability teams at mid-market and enterprise companies
- ESG consultants and auditors managing multiple clients
- Corporate compliance and reporting functions
